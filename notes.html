Mobile-first Design

  • Know your users better than they know themselves.
  • Number of taps affects users' perception of speed.
  • Google: 1/2 second delay = 20% drop in traffic.
  • Bing: 2 second delay = 4% drop in revenue.
  • AOL: Fastest 10% of users stay 50% longer.
  • Remember single-hand position "reachability" for mobile design.
  • For ads on mobile, consider having the ad behind the content and (a) gap(s) in the content so that a small window scrolls past the full ad without consuming screen real estate; it also looks unique and interesting.

Question: What would localStorage be used for in a legit app other than keeping a user's username and maybe a screen arrangement of some sort? It seems that everything else should persist on the server.
